What is this?

Klo stories invites you to explore the fascinating and diverse world of toilets. This project is a first attempt to bring to life the many invisible aspects of this essential part of daily life. As you immerse yourself in this universe, you may find yourself captivated by its complexity, discovering the multitude of facets it encompasses..

Crafted with great care and enthusiasm, this universe aims to shed light on the highly political and social dimensions of toilets. Through a playful and engaging approach, we aim to mediate an empathetic and accessible understanding of these “contested spaces” (Barcan 2010) and provide resources for deeper exploration.

Feel free to get flushed right away and explore the spaces on your own, or use our guidance to better understand how it all works. You can also get more background information on the project, its origins, who we are, and most importantly, why we believe toilets are so significant.
